Indian Knowledge System (IKS) refers to the vast and diverse traditional knowledge developed in India over millennia. It includes disciplines such as philosophy, mathematics, astronomy, medicine, architecture, metallurgy, linguistics, arts, and governance, deeply rooted in texts like the Vedas, Upanishads, and various scientific treatises.
