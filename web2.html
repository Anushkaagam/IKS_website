Core Texts: The Foundational Works

Vedic Texts

The Vedas are the oldest and most authoritative scriptures in the Indian tradition, composed in Sanskrit around 1500-500 BCE.

Upanishads

The Upanishads are philosophical texts that explore the nature of reality, the self (Atman), and the ultimate reality (Brahman)

Puranas

A vast genre of texts narrating the mythology, history, and cosmology of the universe.

Philosophy and Spirituality: The Six Schools

Samkhya

Enumerates elements and the evolution of the universe.

Yoga

Uses techniques for physical and spiritual well-being.

Nyaya

Focuses on logic and methods of valid knowledge.
